AI INTERVIEW ASSISTANT

This is a static website that demonstrates a timed Q&A interview and a dashboard saved locally on your device.

Start as Interviewee Open Dashboard

What’s inside?

  • Interviewee page: profile capture (Name/Email/Phone) + 6 questions with per-difficulty timers.
  • Auto-submit on timeout, scoring (heuristic), and a final summary.
  • Interviewer page: local dashboard, sortable by score/date, with full per-candidate details.
  • All data persists in your browser (localStorage). Close & reopen and it’s still there.

Note: PDF/DOCX extraction is hard in a purely static site. This website asks the candidate for missing fields directly (per the brief) and includes an optional “Paste from resume” box.
